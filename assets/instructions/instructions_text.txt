Try to avoid crashing into oncoming obstacles or walls.
Use [Y] to move to the left and [M] to move to the right.
Sometimes you will not have full control over the spaceship.
You will encounter drift that will push you in either direction
as well as input noise that will make your steering inconsistent.
Before each different section in the level, the walls become narrower.
Here you must steer to the horizontal center of the screen to avoid crashing.
You will now get the opportunity to train a bit. 
After a successful training run the experiment will start.
Good luck pilot!
